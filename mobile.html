Development

Custom & Templated Storyboards

Reguardless of your app type or purpose, all development follows the same essential path. After refining the idea, Reverb will collaberate with you to craft an initial storyboard [fig.1]

After constructing a storyboard, or flow chart, Reverb will begin the process of converting that story into wireframe screens. Wireframes are bare bone Mockups of screens that illustrate the size, placement, and function of the elements within that screen.

After the Wireframe process is finalized, Reverb begins the design process. In this step we design visually functional mockups stylized to suit your needs. When completed, a prototype is generated. Although not a functional version of the app, the user experience and click through is identical, providing a convienient and more affordable way to test user appeal, and share with colleagues.
