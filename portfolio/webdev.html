OKGO An App Apart

Process

Developing OKGO took an amalgem of skillsets; engineers, data analysts, and UI specialists.

At the end of the day, we learned the user experience was paramount to the success of the location discovery style application.

Combining dozens of API's, everything from wearables, to taxi positions, to geo-tweets, to public-transit, to bike sharing programs. Patterns emerge, Data clarifies, and applications diversify.
